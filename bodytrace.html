Weight Tracking: BodyTrace Scale

Enable BodyTrace scale to transmit weight to the HealthSlate app

The Body Trace scale arrives synced to your HealthSlate account right from the box. The only thing you need to do is weigh yourself on the scale and your weights will be transmitted to the HealthSlate app.

Er 3 OLd BodyTrace Scale Error Message

Er 3 OLd displays typically when the user weight exceeds the scale capacity.

The BodyTrace scale maximum weight that it measures is 397 lb.

Er 4 CELL BodyTrace Scale Error Message

Er 4 CELL means that no cellular network can be found. Move the scale to another location in your home and weigh again.

Er 4 bAtt BodyTrace Scale Error Message

Er 4 bAtt means the batteries in the BodyTrace scale are low or need to be replaced.

Er 5 Conn BodyTrace Scale Error Message

Er 5 Conn error message means the scale is struggling to find a strong enough signal. Re-weigh yourself or move the scale to a different location in your home and re-weigh.

The scale uses nearby cell towers to transmit the data. Place the scale in a location of your home that has optimal cell phone reception. No Wi-Fi or Bluetooth is required and your cell phone is not used to transmit the signal.

When you stand on your scale you should see your weight, followed by this animation:

Then a rectangle will display, indicating the weight has transmitted successfully.

Recalibrate BodyTrace Scale

Please remove the batteries from the scale for at least 60 minutes and then insert the batteries back into the scale, this will cause the scale to recalibrate and your scale should display an accurate weight after.
