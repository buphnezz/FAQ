Weight Tracking: BodyTrace Scale

How do I sync my BodyTrace scale with the HealthSlate app?

The BodyTrace scale arrives synced to your HealthSlate account right from the box. The only thing you need to do is weigh yourself on the scale and your weight will be transmitted to the HealthSlate app.

How do I fix an "Er 3 OLd" BodyTrace scale error message?

"Er 3 OLd" displays when the user weight exceeds the scale capacity.
The BodyTrace scale maximum weight that it measured is 397 lb.

How do I fix an "Er 4 CELL" BodyTrace scale error message?

"Er 4 CELL" means that no cellular network can be found. Move the scale to another location in your home and weigh again.

How do I fix an "Er 4 bAtt" BodyTrace scale error message?

"Er 4 bAtt" means the batteries in the BodyTrace scale are low or need to be replaced.

How do I fix an "Er 5 Conn" BodyTrace Scale Error Message?

"Er 5 Conn" means the scale is struggling to find a strong enough signal. Re-weigh yourself or move the scale to a different location in your home and re-weigh.

The scale uses nearby cell towers to transmit the data. Place the scale in a location of your home that has optimal cell phone reception. No Wi-Fi or Bluetooth is required and your cell phone is not used to transmit the signal.

Then a rectangle will display, indicating the weight has transmitted successfully.

How do I recalibrate my BodyTrace scale?

Please remove the batteries from the scale for at least 60 minutes and then insert the batteries back into the scale. This will cause the scale to recalibrate and your scale should display an accurate weight after.
