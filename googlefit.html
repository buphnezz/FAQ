Physical Activity and Tracking: Google Fit

Enable Google Fit app to track steps on HealthSlate app (Android Devices only)

  1. Download and install the Google Fit app from the Play Store.
  2. Log in to Google Fit and link a Google account.

  3. From the HealthSlate app home screen select the menu icon in the top left corner of the screen.

  4. Select Settings.
  5. Select Manage Device Sync.
  6. Select Enable Google Fit.
Google Fit steps are not displaying on, or syncing to the HealthSlate app appropriately (Android Devices only)

  1. From the HealthSlate app home screen select the menu icon in the top left corner of the screen.

  2. Select Manage Device Sync.
  3. You should see the words Disable Google Fit, which means you've already enabled Google Fit. Select Disable Google Fit and then select Enable Google Fit.
  4. The app may ask for permissions. Be sure to choose the same Gmail account that you used to set up your Google fit account and select Allow. This allows the HealthSlate app to receive steps from Google Fit.
