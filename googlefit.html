Physical Activity and Tracking: Google Fit

Five things you can do to resolve your Google Fit step syncing issue
Note: The Google Fit steps do not update live to the HealthSlate app and there is a delay with the steps updating. The steps should catch up by the end of the day.
  1. Confirm your Google Fit steps are displaying from the Google Fit app.
    1. Open Google Fit app
    2. Are there steps displayed from your Google Fit app home screen?
  2. If your Google Fit steps do not display from the Google Fit app, navigate to: Google Fit support
  3. If your steps display from your Google Fit app but do not display from the HealthSlate app, confirm your HealthSlate account email address matches your Google Fit account email address.
    1. Open Google Fit app
    2. Select the menu in the top left corner of the screen.
    3. Select Settings
    4. Scroll down and confirm the email under Account matches the email used to login to the HealthSlate app.
  4. If your Google Fit steps still do not show from the HealthSlate app, disable and re-enable Google Fit from the HealthSlate app.
    1. From the HealthSlate app home screen select the menu icon in the top left corner of the screen.
    2. Select Settings.
    3. Select Manage Device Sync.
    4. Select Disable Google Fit.
    5. Select Enable Google Fit.
  5. If the above solutions do not resolve your issue, please contact HealthSlate tech support.
How do I enable Google Fit to track steps on the HealthSlate app?

  1. Download and install the Google Fit app from the Play Store.
  2. Log in to Google Fit and link a Google account.

  3. From the HealthSlate app home screen select the menu icon in the top left corner of the screen.

  4. Select Settings.
  5. Select Manage Device Sync.
  6. Select Enable Google Fit.
