Physical Activity and Tracking: Apple HealthKit

Enable apple HealthKit app to track steps on HealthSlate app (iOS devices only)

  1. From the HealthSlate app home screen, select More .

  2. Select Settings.
  3. Select Manage Device Sync.
  4. Select Enable HealthKit, this will allow your iPhone to track your steps.
Apple HealthKit Steps Are Not Displaying/Syncing

  1. From the HealthSlate app home screen, select More .

  2. Select Settings.
  3. Select Manage Device Sync.
  4. Select Disable HealthKit. This will allow your iPhone to track your steps.
  5. Select Enable HealthKit, this will allow your iPhone to track your steps.
Enable apple watch to track steps on HealthSlate app

  1. From the HealthSlate app home screen, select More .

  2. Select Settings.
  3. Select Manage Device Sync.
  4. Select Enable HealthKit, this will allow your Apple Watch to track your steps.
