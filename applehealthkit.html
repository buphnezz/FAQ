How do I track activity with my iPhone?

Four things you can do to resolve your Apple HealthKit step syncing issue
Note: The Apple HealthKit steps do not update live to the HealthSlate app and there is a delay with the steps updating. The steps should catch up by the end of the day.
  1. Confirm your steps are displaying from your Apple HealthKit app.
    1. Select the Health app

    2. Select Health Data near the bottom of the screen

    3. Select Activity.
    4. Select Steps.
    5. Are your steps displayed from this screen?
  2. If your Apple HealthKit steps do not display from the Health app, navigate to: Apple HealthKit support

  3. If your steps display from your Health app but do not display from the HealthSlate app, Confirm the appropriate permissions are granted.
    1. Select Settings from your iOS device
    2. Select Privacy.
    3. Select Health.
    4. Select HealthSlate.
    5. Enable Steps, Weights, and Workouts.
  4. If your steps still do not display from the HealthSlate app, disable and re-enable Apple HealthKit from the HealthSlate app.
    1. Select More

    2. Select Settings.
    3. Select Manage Device Sync.
    4. Select Disable HealthKit.
    5. Select Enable HealthKit, this will allow your iPhone to track your steps.
  5. If the above solutions do not resolve your issue, please contact HealthSlate tech support.
How do I set up Apple HealthKit with HealthSlate?

  1. From the HealthSlate app home screen, select More

  2. Select Settings.
  3. Select Manage Device Sync.
  4. Select Enable HealthKit.
How do I set up my Apple Watch to sync to the HealthSlate app?

  1. From the HealthSlate app home screen, select More

  2. Select Settings.
  3. Select Manage Device Sync.
  4. Select Enable HealthKit, this will allow your Apple Watch to track your steps.
