Other Questions

When will I receive the FitBit coupon?
  • Fitbit email coupons are sent to members who have been meaningfully engaged in the HealthSlate program during their first 4 weeks, and is typically sent between weeks 4 and 6 of the HealthSlate program. The email is sent from “support@solera4me.com” and the subject line will read "Redeem Your Fitbit Now."
When do I receive my scale?
  • The scale should arrive around 10 business days after joining the HealthSlate program.
How do I fix the sound when I watch a video on my iPhone?
  • Please move the switch on the side of the phone out of do not disturb mode. You should now hear the volume play during the videos.

How do I install the HealthSlate app on my iPhone or iPad?
  1. From your iPad, select HealthSlate app or copy and paste the link http:HealthSlate.com/app into your iPad's internet browser.
  2. You will then be directed to the appropriate app store and instructed to download and install our HealthSlate app.
What do I do when I am unable to select "GET" when downloading the HealthSlate app on my iPhone or iPad?
  1. You may be unable to download the HealthSlate app because your phone has settings that are restricting the app based on rating. Please follow these instructions:
  2. Select General.
  3. Select Restrictions.
  4. From the Allowed Content: section, select Apps.
  5. Select 12+ or select Allow All Apps.
How to I access HealthSlate from my computer?
  • Please visit https://diabetes.healthslate.com/consumer/login from your web browser to access the HealthSlate from your computer.
How much does HealthSlate cost me?
  • The HealthSlate program is covered 100% by your insurance provider at no cost to you.
Can I connect MyFitnessPal with HealthSlate?
  • The HealthSlate app is unable to connect to or share data with MyFitnessPal.
