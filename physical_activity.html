Step Related Issues

Installing Fitbit on SmartPhone

  1. "Download and install the Fitbit app on your phone. Ensure Bluetooth is enabled.
  2. Sync your Fitbit device to the Fitbit app, please select Account from the home screen of your Fitbit app.
  3. Select Set up a Device.
  4. Select the Fitbit device you have (ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone).

    5. For any further assistance setting up your Fitbit device or account, please Contact Fitbit Support

  5. Open the HealthSlate app.
  6. From the HealthSlate app home screen:
    1. iOS device: select More Inserting image... and select Settings.
    2. Android device: select the menu Inserting image... icon in the top left corner of the screen and select Settings.
    3. Select Manage Fitbit devices.
    4. Check the box next to Fitbit and select Authorize
    5. Enter your email and password that you used with your Fitbit app and select Log In, to confirm the authorization process.
  7. Select Manage Device Sync.

Installing Fitbit on Mac or PC

Fitbit has a quick article on how to setup your Fitbit device on their website. Select the link and scroll down, you'll see directions for setting up your Fitbit with phones, tablets, Windows computers and a MAC.
Track Steps with iPhone/Setting up Apple HealthKit.

  1. From the HealthSlate app home screen, select More
  2. Select Settings.
  3. Select Manage Device Sync.
  4. Select Enable HealthKit, this will allow your iPhone to track your steps.
Track Steps with Android/Setting up Google Fit
  1. Download and install the Google Fit app from the Play Store.
  2. Log into Google Fit and link a Google account.
  3. From the HealthSlate app home screen select the menu icon in the top left corner of the screen.
  4. Select Settings.
  5. Select Manage Device Sync.
  6. Select Enable Google Fit.
Receiving Your Fitbit Email Coupon
Fitbit email coupons are sent to members who have been meaningfully engaged in the HealthSlate program during their first 4 weeks, and is typically sent between weeks 4 and 6 of the HealthSlate program. The email is sent from “support@solera4me.com” and the subject line will read "Redeem Your Fitbit Now."
Google Fit Steps Are Not Displaying/Syncing
  1. From the HealthSlate app home screen select the menu icon in the top left corner of the screen and select Settings.
  2. Select Manage Device Sync.
  3. You should see the words Disable Google Fit, which means you've already enabled Google Fit. Select Disable Google Fit and then select Enable Google Fit.
  4. The app may ask for permissions. Be sure to choose the same Gmail account that you used to set up your Google fit account and select Allow. This allows the HealthSlate app to receive steps from Google Fit.
Apple HealthKit Steps Are Not Displaying/Syncing
  1. From the HealthSlate app home screen, select More .
  2. Select Settings.
  3. Select Manage Device Sync.
  4. Select Disable HealthKit. This will allow your iPhone to track your steps.
  5. Select Enable HealthKit this will allow your iPhone to track your steps.
Step Calculation While Participating in the Step Challenge
The Step Challenge displays the average number of steps taken since Monday.

The HealthSlate app is only compatible with Google Fit, Apple HealthKit and FitBit.
