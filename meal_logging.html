Meal Logging

How do I input a meal using "Free Form Text?"
  1. From the HealthSlate app home screen:
    1. iOS device: select the button.

    2. Android device: select the button.
  2. Select Meals.
  3. Select the meal you would like to log.
  4. Choose your meal source.
  5. Log your meal using "Type, Talk, Search".
  6. Select the "Tap to speak" button to speak the items you consumed for your meal and select Submit.
  7. The items and their respective calories should be displayed, scroll to the bottom of the screen and select Done.
How do I set up a "Go-To" Meal?
  1. From the HealthSlate home screen:
    1. iOS device: select the Food icon on the bottom of the screen.

    2. Android device: select the Food icon from the top of the screen.
  2. Until you have set your Go-To meal, you will have a button that says "Add Go-To Meal."
  3. Once Your "Go-To" is set you will have a picture of that meal for a quick way to log your meal.
  4. You can change your Go-To meal by pressing the Change "Go-To" meal link and choosing a new "Go-To" meal.

How do I favorite a meal?
  1. To favorite a meal, from the HealthSlate home screen:
    1. iOS device: select the Food icon on the bottom of the screen.
    2. Android device: select the Food icon from the top of the screen.
  2. Navigate to the meal you would like to favorite and select the meal.
  3. Select the star icon directly below the image of the meal.
How do I delete a meal?
  1. From the HealthSlate home screen, scroll down and select Log Book.
    1. iOS device: select the Meals tab near the top of the screen.

    2. Android device: select Meal from the drop down menu near the top right corner of the screen.
  2. Navigate to, and select the meal you'd like to delete.
  3. Select Delete Meal in the lower right corner of the screen.
How do I set up a calorie budget?

  1. From the HealthSlate home screen:
    1. iOS Device: select More

    2. Android Device: select the menu
  2. select Settings
  3. Select Calorie Budget and Logging.
  4. Select Daily Calorie Budget and input your new Daily Calorie Budgetand select Save.

How do I edit my Past or Favorite meal?

  • When you choose to log a past favorite meal, it is quick and easy. You choose your meal and you are navigated back to the MEALS screen.
    But what do you do when you want to edit the Past or Favorite Meals?
  1. Select the meal you want to edit.
  2. From the Meal Details screen you can select the pencil icon to edit the image, the meal (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snack) or the meal source.
    Click Add/Edit item(s) at the bottom left of the screen to add or edit the food items.
  3. Food items can be added simply by typing, removed by pressing the grey x or edited by pressing the grey pencil icon. To change the calories, servings, bring up the Food Details screen.
  4. From the Food Details screen, you have the ability to change Item, Qty, unit, calories, carbs, fat, protein. It is all up to you.
    When you are done select Save.
  5. When done adjusting the meal, select Log It.
  6. When satisfied with your meal logging, select Done.
