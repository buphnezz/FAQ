Installing Fitbit on SmartPhone

  1. "Download and install the Fitbit app on your phone. Ensure Bluetooth is enabled.
  2. Sync your Fitbit device to the Fitbit app, please select Account from the home screen of your Fitbit app.
  3. Select Set up a Device.
  4. Select the Fitbit device you have (ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone).

    5. For any further assistance setting up your Fitbit device or account, please Contact Fitbit Support

  5. Open the HealthSlate app.
  6. From the HealthSlate app home screen:
    1. iOS device: select More Inserting image... and select Settings.
    2. Android device: select the menu Inserting image... icon in the top left corner of the screen and select Settings.
    3. Select Manage Fitbit devices.
    4. Check the box next to Fitbit and select Authorize
    5. Enter your email and password that you used with your Fitbit app and select Log In, to confirm the authorization process.
  7. Select Manage Device Sync.

Installing the HealthSlate app on iPad

  1. From your iPad, select http:HealthSlate.com/app or copy and paste the link into your iPad's internet browser.



  2. You will then be directed to the appropriate app store and instructed to download and install our HealthSlate app.

Unable to download HealthSlate from app store

  1. You may be unable to download the HealthSlate app because your phone has settings that are restricting the app based on rating. Please follow these instructions:


  2. Select General.


  3. Select Restrictions.


  4. From the Allowed Content: section, select Apps.


  5. Select 12+ or select Allow All Apps.
